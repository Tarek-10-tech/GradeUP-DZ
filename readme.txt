# Grade Calculator DZ

Grade Calculator DZ is an open-source app that helps Algerian students calculate their final grades based on subject coefficients and exam scores.

## Features

- Customize subject coefficients based on level and specialty
- Instant final average calculation
- Works offline
- No ads, no tracking, no data collection

## How to use

1. Enter your grades and coefficients
2. The app automatically calculates your average
3. Adjust your inputs freely
